Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can browse our products on the Products page, select items, and submit an inquiry. Our sales team will contact you with pricing and delivery options.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order quantities vary by product. Please contact our sales team for specific MOQ details. We also offer bulk discounts for large orders.

Do you provide technical support?

Yes, we provide comprehensive technical support including application guidance, dosage recommendations, and troubleshooting assistance.

What are your delivery options?

We offer delivery across India with options for standard and express shipping. Delivery timelines depend on location and product availability.

Are your products certified?

Yes, all our products are manufactured following international standards and are certified by relevant agricultural authorities in India.

Yes, we conduct regular training programs for farmers on proper product usage, pest management, and sustainable farming practices.
